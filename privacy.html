Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

1. Who we are

Pawprints ("the app", "we") is operated by the developer ("themintai") for personal use to record memories about pets. Contact: support@themintai.com.

2. What we collect

To run the app we collect and store the following information:

  • Account — email address, display name, profile photo URL if you sign in with Google. Held by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Pets & memories — pet names, types, photos, captions, dates of memories, milestones, vaccines, vet visits, medication, weight entries. Stored in Google Firestore.
  • Generated videos — the MP4 files Pawprints renders from your selected memories, plus a thumbnail image. Stored in Google Cloud Storage under your user id.
  • Subscription state — whether you have an active Pawprints+ subscription. We do not see or store your payment details — RevenueCat and Google Play handle payment.
  • Diagnostic data — anonymized crash reports and basic device info via Sentry. No personal content of your memories is sent to Sentry.

3. How we use it

Solely to run the app: render your slideshows, sync your timeline across devices, enforce free-tier limits, and diagnose crashes. We do not sell your data. We do not show advertising.

4. Where it is stored

Firestore and Cloud Storage in europe-west3 (Frankfurt, Germany). Cloud Run rendering service in the same region. Auth and crash data are stored on Google and Sentry's infrastructure.

5. Third-party processors

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Run, Cloud Functions)
  • RevenueCat (subscription state)
  • Google Play Billing (payments)
  • Sentry (crash reporting)

6. Your rights

You can:

  • Access — see everything we hold about you in-app: pets, timeline, videos.
  • Delete — Settings → Delete account permanently deletes your account and every photo, memory, and video associated with it. Removal is immediate and irreversible.
  • Export — email us for a copy of your data (currently not yet automated in-app).
  • Withdraw consent — sign out at any time. Stop using the app and request account deletion.

7. Children

Pawprints is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Changes

Material changes to this policy will be announced in the app and on this page.
