Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

1. Who we are

Pawprints ("the app", "we") is operated by themintai ("the developer") for personal use to record memories about pets. Contact: support@themintai.com.

2. What we collect

To run the app we collect and store the following information:

  • Account — email address, display name, and profile photo URL if you sign in with Google. Held by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Pets & memories — pet names, types, photos, captions, dates of memories, milestones, vaccines, vet visits, medication, and weight entries. Stored in Google Firestore.
  • Generated videos — the MP4 files Pawprints renders from your selected memories, plus a thumbnail image. Stored in Google Cloud Storage under your user id.
  • Subscription state — whether you have an active Pawprints+ subscription. We do not see or store your payment details — RevenueCat and Google Play handle payment.
  • Diagnostic data — anonymized crash reports and basic device info (operating system version, device model, app version, anonymous installation identifier) via Sentry. No personal content of your memories is sent to Sentry.

3. How we use it

Solely to run the app: render your slideshows, sync your timeline across devices, enforce free-tier limits, and diagnose crashes. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with brokers.

4. Where it is stored

Firestore and Cloud Storage in europe-west3 (Frankfurt, Germany). The Cloud Run rendering service runs in the same region. Authentication is held by Google Firebase. Anonymized crash data is processed by Sentry.

5. Third-party processors

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Run, Cloud Functions)
  • RevenueCat (subscription state management; no payment details, no personal content)
  • Google Play Billing (payment processing)
  • Sentry (anonymized crash reporting)
  • fal.ai — only used when you opt in to a Pawprints+ AI video feature. See Section 9.

6. No advertising

Pawprints does not display third-party advertisements. We do not sell your personal data or content. We do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers. The free tier is supported entirely by the optional Pawprints+ subscription.

7. Data security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. User content (pet photos, generated videos, timeline entries) is stored in Google Firebase with security rules that restrict reads and writes to the authenticated account that owns them. Inter-service calls from our Firebase Functions trigger to the Cloud Run rendering service use Google-signed OIDC bearer tokens scoped to that service; the renderer rejects all unauthenticated traffic at the Google Cloud platform layer. Stored data is encrypted at rest by Google Cloud. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100 % secure, however, and you accept that residual risk when you use the app.

8. External links and sharing

The app may take you to external destinations — for example, when you share a generated reel to WhatsApp, Instagram, your phone's Photos library, or any other app via the operating system's share sheet at your request. Once content leaves Pawprints, the privacy practices of the receiving service apply. We are not responsible for what those services do with the shared content after you send it.

9. AI features (Pawprints+)

Pawprints+ includes optional AI-powered video features ("Living Photos" and "Imagined Reel") which are not yet released. When these ship, the photos you explicitly select for an AI rendering will be sent to fal.ai (our AI provider) over a secure connection. fal.ai uses your photos for the immediate processing request only and does not retain them beyond their stated processing window. We will surface a clear "AI-generated" disclosure on any reel that used these features so that the result is never mistaken for an unedited photograph. AI features are entirely opt-in: the free Slideshow tier never sends your photos outside our infrastructure.

10. Your rights

You can:

  • Access — see everything we hold about you in-app: pets, timeline, videos.
  • Delete — open Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your authentication record and every photo, memory, and video associated with it. Removal is immediate and irreversible.
  • Export — email us at support@themintai.com for a copy of your data. (Self-serve export is on our roadmap but not yet in-app.)
  • Withdraw consent — sign out at any time. Stop using the app and request account deletion.

11. Children

Pawprints is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact support@themintai.com and we will remove it promptly.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app and posted here with a revised "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
